                Dose                                                              
                1. A measure of the energy deposited by radiation in a target.    
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                2. Absorbed dose, committed equivalent dose, committed effective dose,
                                                                                  
                   effective dose, equivalent dose or organ dose, as indicated by the con-
                   text.                                                          
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                Dose assessment                                                   
                                                                                  
                Assessment of the dose(s) to an individual or group of people.    
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                Dose constraint                                                   
                                                                                  
                A prospective and radiation source related value of individual dose that is
                used in planned exposure situations as a parameter for the optimization
                                                                                  
                of protection and safety for the source, and that serves as a boundary in
                                                                                  
                defining the range of options in optimization.                    
                                                                                  
                                                                                  
                Dose limit                                                        
                The value of a quantity used in certain specified activities or circumstances
                                                                                  
                that must not be exceeded.
